The Challenge

Manual Reporting Bottlenecks

A growing IT services company was managing project tracking, resource allocation, timesheets, and client reporting through multiple complex Excel spreadsheets.

Every Week:

  • Project managers spent hours manually updating weekly progress reports
  • Team leads consolidated raw files from multiple departments
  • Executive leadership waited days for consolidated business insights
  • Frequent manual reporting errors created delays in project decisions

The company was spending over 30 hours per week on manual reporting alone.

Automation Workflow Architecture (Click to enlarge)
The Solution

Custom Automation Ecosystem

We developed a custom automation ecosystem tailored to consolidate departmental pipelines, streamline timesheet data, and auto-generate executive dashboards in minutes.

  • Excel & Google Macros Automation: Custom scripts run consolidation routines in seconds.
  • Automated MIS Reporting: Instant distribution queues format files correctly.
  • Project Tracking Dashboard: Real-time status views prevent delivery slipups.
  • Resource Utilization Dashboard: Instant tracking of employee hours vs client billing.
  • Automated Data Consolidation Workflows: Links accounting software directly with management reporting.
