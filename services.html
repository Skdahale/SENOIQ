FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does implementation take?

Simple chatbot and basic workflow automations can be deployed in 2-3 weeks. Larger, custom AI agents and enterprise workflow integrations typically take 4-6 weeks depending on complexity.

Can you integrate with existing software?

Yes, we integrate AI solutions with CRM systems (HubSpot, Salesforce), ERPs, communication channels (Slack, WhatsApp, Teams), email clients, and standard APIs.

Do you provide support after launch?

Absolutely. We offer dedicated post-launch monitoring, maintenance, optimization, and monthly support retainer packages to ensure your systems evolve with your business.

Can AI work with my existing workflow?

Definitely. Our approach is to integrate AI as an assistant to your existing team, working alongside your current software tools and operational procedures rather than replacing them.
