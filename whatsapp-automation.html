WhatsApp Automation & Appointment Scheduling

The Challenge

Reception Workload & Missed Calls

Reception staff at the clinic were spending the majority of their workdays manually answering repetitive patient inquiries. This administrative overhead resulted in delayed responses and missed customer queries.

Key Roadblocks:

  • Staff tied up manually answering repetitive appointment & pricing questions
  • Delays in checking and coordinating doctor availability schedules
  • High volume of missed inquiries during busy peak clinic hours
  • Delayed follow-up request responses, averaging 45 minutes
  • No structured automated reminder sequence for scheduled patient visits

The Solution

AI WhatsApp Assistant Hub

We built a complete WhatsApp Business API integration that handles customer FAQs, synchronizes appointments in real-time with clinic schedules, and dispatches automated reminder sequences.

  • AI WhatsApp Assistant: Deploys responsive 24/7 conversational receptionists.
  • Booking Automation: Integrates synced doctor availability calendar systems.
  • FAQ Automation: Automatically resolves pricing and clinic info questions.
  • Follow-Up Reminders: Triggers drip booking reminder campaigns.
